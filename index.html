The Public Astronomical Observatory at Junta House, Panjim

The P.A.O. Panjim was set up by Association of Friends of Astronomy (Goa)in 1990 and is supported by the Department of Science, Technology & Environment (Govt. of Goa). It is the first public observatory to be set up in India. It is voluntarily managed by the members of Association of Friends of Astronomy (Goa) and all activities are funded by the Government.
